Tomato Rice

30 mins Easy Vegetarian
Flavorful Tomato Rice

A quick and delicious one-pot meal that combines the tangy flavor of tomatoes with aromatic spices and fluffy rice. Perfect for busy weekdays or as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 4 large Tomatoes
  • 1 large Onion
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 2 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare Base

    Heat oil or ghee in a pot and add whole spices.

  2. 2

    Saute Onions

    Add onions and saute until translucent.

  3. 3

    Add Aromatics

    Add ginger-garlic paste and saute until fragrant.

  4. 4

    Cook Tomatoes

    Add tomatoes and cook until mushy.

  5. 5

    Add Spices

    Add ground spices and mix well.

  6. 6

    Cook Rice

    Add rice and water, then cook until rice is done.

  7. 7

    Finish

    Fluff up the rice and serve hot.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, add a pinch of asafoetida (hing) while tempering.
  • You can add vegetables like carrots and peas for a more nutritious meal.
  • For a spicier version, increase the amount of red chili powder.
