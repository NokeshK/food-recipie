Buddha Bowl

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 20 mins (grains/veg) Serves: 2

Nutritious and Colorful

Buddha bowls are a fantastic way to pack a variety of healthy ingredients into one satisfying meal. This recipe provides a basic framework, but feel free to mix and match your favorite grains, vegetables, and protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Cooked quinoa or brown rice
  • 1 cup Roasted sweet potatoes, cubed
  • 1 cup Steamed broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup Edamame, shelled
  • 1/2 cup Shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup Sliced avocado
  • Optional protein Grilled tofu, chickpeas, or chicken
  • For dressing Tahini-lemon dressing or peanut dressing

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare Base and Vegetables

    Cook your chosen grain (quinoa or brown rice) according to package instructions. Roast sweet potatoes until tender. Steam broccoli florets until crisp-tender. Prepare any other vegetables or protein.

  2. 2

    Assemble the Bowl

    Divide the cooked grain between two bowls. Arrange the roasted sweet potatoes, steamed broccoli, edamame, shredded carrots, and sliced avocado on top of the grain in separate sections.

  3. 3

    Add Protein (Optional)

    If using, add your cooked protein (tofu, chickpeas, chicken) to the bowl.

  4. 4

    Drizzle with Dressing

    Drizzle your desired dressing generously over the contents of the bowl.

  5. 5

    Serve

    Serve immediately and enjoy your colorful and nutritious Buddha bowl!

Tips & Notes

  • Meal prep the components (grains, roasted vegetables) at the beginning of the week for quick assembly.
  • Experiment with different dressings like a peanut sauce or a simple vinaigrette.
  • Add a sprinkle of sesame seeds or chopped nuts for extra texture.
