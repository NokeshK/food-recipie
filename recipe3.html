Fresh Garden Salad

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 0 mins Serves: 2-4

Crisp and Healthy

This fresh garden salad is simple, healthy, and customizable. Use whatever fresh vegetables you have on hand for a quick and nutritious meal or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups Mixed greens
  • 1 cup Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup Red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Carrots, shredded
  • Optional Bell peppers, radishes, or other vegetables
  • For dressing: Olive oil, vinegar (or lemon juice), salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare Vegetables

    Wash and prepare all the vegetables. Tear or chop the lettuce as needed. Halve the cherry tomatoes, slice the cucumber and red onion, and shred the carrots.

  2. 2

    Combine Ingredients

    In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, carrots, and any other vegetables you are using.

  3. 3

    Make Dressing

    In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar (or lemon juice), salt, and pepper. Adjust proportions to your taste.

  4. 4

    Dress and Serve

    Pour the dressing over the salad just before serving. Toss gently to coat all the vegetables. Serve immediately.

Tips & Notes

  • Add protein like grilled chicken, fish, or beans to make it a complete meal.
  • Top with nuts, seeds, or croutons for added crunch.
  • Experiment with different types of lettuce and vegetables for variety.
