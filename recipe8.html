Thai Curry

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 30 mins Serves: 4

Spicy and Aromatic

A vibrant and flavorful Thai curry that can be customized with your favorite protein and vegetables. The blend of spices and creamy coconut milk creates a truly satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Vegetable oil
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1-3 tbsp Red or green curry paste (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) Full-fat coconut milk
  • 1 cup Vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp Fish sauce (or soy sauce for vegetarian)
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 2 cups Mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, etc.), chopped
  • 1 cup Protein (sliced chicken, tofu, or shrimp)
  • Fresh basil or cilantro For garnish
  • Cooked rice For serving

Instructions

  1. 1

    Sauté Aromatics

    Heat oil in a large pot or wok over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add minced garlic and curry paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  2. 2

    Add Liquids and Seasoning

    Pour in the coconut milk and broth, stirring to combine with the curry paste. Add fish sauce and sugar. Bring to a simmer.

  3. 3

    Cook Protein and Vegetables

    Add your chosen protein and mixed vegetables to the pot. Simmer until the protein is cooked through and the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 10-15 minutes.

  4. 4

    Adjust Seasoning

    Taste the curry and adjust seasoning as needed. You may add more fish sauce, sugar, or curry paste to reach your desired flavor and spice level.

  5. 5

    Serve

    Serve the Thai curry hot over cooked rice. Garnish with fresh basil or cilantro.

Tips & Notes

  • Use good quality curry paste for the best flavor.
  • Don't overcrowd the pot; cook in batches if necessary.
  • For a thicker curry, simmer uncovered for the last few minutes.
