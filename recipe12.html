Egg Curry

40 mins Easy Vegetarian
Dhaba Style Egg Curry

A delicious and protein-rich egg curry that's perfect for a quick and satisfying meal. This dhaba-style recipe brings authentic Indian flavors to your table.

Ingredients

  • 6 Boiled Eggs
  • 2 large Onions
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/4 cup Cream (optional)
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare Eggs

    Fry boiled eggs until golden brown and set aside.

  2. 2

    Saute Onions

    In the same pan, saute onions until translucent.

  3. 3

    Add Aromatics

    Add ginger-garlic paste and saute until fragrant.

  4. 4

    Cook Tomatoes

    Add tomatoes and cook until mushy.

  5. 5

    Add Spices

    Add all the ground spices and mix well.

  6. 6

    Simmer

    Add water and simmer until gravy thickens.

  7. 7

    Add Eggs

    Add fried eggs and simmer for a few minutes.

  8. 8

    Finish

    Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or bread.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, make small slits in the boiled eggs before frying.
  • You can add a pinch of sugar to balance the flavors.
  • For a creamier texture, add a splash of cream at the end.
