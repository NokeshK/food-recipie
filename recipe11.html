Chicken Curry

45 mins Medium Non-Veg
Traditional Indian Chicken Curry

A flavorful and aromatic chicken curry that's perfect for any occasion. This traditional Indian dish combines tender chicken pieces with a rich, spiced gravy that will tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken
  • 2 large Onions
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt (optional)
  • 1/2 cup Coconut Milk (optional)
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare the Base

    Heat oil in a pan and saute onions until golden brown.

  2. 2

    Add Aromatics

    Add ginger-garlic paste and saute until fragrant.

  3. 3

    Cook Tomatoes

    Add tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.

  4. 4

    Add Spices

    Add all the ground spices and mix well.

  5. 5

    Cook Chicken

    Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.

  6. 6

    Simmer

    Add water or coconut milk and simmer until chicken is cooked through.

  7. 7

    Finish

    Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or bread.

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, marinate the chicken in yogurt and spices for 30 minutes before cooking.
  • Adjust the spice levels according to your preference.
  • You can add vegetables like potatoes or carrots for a more wholesome meal.
