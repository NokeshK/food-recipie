Berry Smoothie

Prep: 5 mins Cook: 5 mins Serves: 2

Refreshing and Nutritious

A delicious and healthy berry smoothie that's perfect for breakfast or as a refreshing snack. Packed with antioxidants and vitamins, this smoothie is both nutritious and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup Almond milk
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1/2 cup Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare Ingredients

    Wash and hull the strawberries. Peel and slice the banana.

  2. 2

    Blend Base

    Add yogurt, almond milk, and honey to the blender first.

  3. 3

    Add Fruits

    Add the mixed berries and banana to the blender.

  4. 4

    Blend Until Smooth

    Add ice cubes and blend until smooth and creamy.

  5. 5

    Serve

    Pour into glasses and serve immediately.

Tips & Notes

  • For a thicker smoothie, add more frozen fruits or less liquid.
  • You can substitute almond milk with any other milk of your choice.
  • Add a scoop of protein powder for an extra protein boost.
