Tomato Soup

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 25-30 mins Serves: 4

Creamy and Comforting

A warm and comforting bowl of classic tomato soup is perfect for a chilly day. This recipe is easy to make and results in a rich and flavorful soup.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Olive oil or butter
  • 1 Medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) Crushed tomatoes
  • 4 cups Vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tsp Sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • 1/2 cup Heavy cream or milk (optional, for creaminess)
  • Fresh basil Chopped, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper To taste

Instructions

  1. 1

    Sauté Aromatics

    Heat olive oil or butter in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute more until fragrant.

  2. 2

    Add Tomatoes and Broth

    Stir in the crushed tomatoes and vegetable or chicken broth. Add sugar, if using. Bring the mixture to a simmer, scraping the bottom of the pot to loosen any browned bits.

  3. 3

    Simmer

    Reduce heat to low, cover, and let the soup simmer for at least 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  4. 4

    Blend (Optional)

    For a smoother soup, carefully use an immersion blender to blend the soup until creamy. Alternatively, transfer the soup in batches to a blender (vent the lid!) and blend until smooth.

  5. 5

    Finish and Serve

    Stir in heavy cream or milk, if desired, and heat through (do not boil). Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.

Tips & Notes

  • Use good quality canned tomatoes for the best flavor.
  • Serve with grilled cheese sandwiches for a classic pairing.
  • You can add other vegetables like carrots or celery along with the onion for more depth of flavor.
